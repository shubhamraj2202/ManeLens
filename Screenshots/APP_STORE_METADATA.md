## Description (4000 chars max — use the version below, ~170 words)

Ever wonder what you'd look like with a different hairstyle — before committing to the scissors?

Hair Lens - AI uses cutting-edge AI to show you a photorealistic preview of any hairstyle on your own photo. Unlike filters that plaster a wig overlay on top, Hair Lens preserves your face, skin tone, and identity — only the hair changes.

**Perfect for:**
• Brides planning their wedding look — browse Indian bridal styles before your big day
• Anyone before a big salon appointment — show your stylist exactly what you want
• Exploring bold cuts, color, or Japanese salon trends without the risk

**How it works:**
1. Take or upload a selfie
2. Browse our curated style catalog — wedding, salon, casual, and bold looks
3. Get a photorealistic AI preview in seconds

Your face stays your face. Just a new hairstyle — instantly.

Start with 3 free previews. No subscription required.
